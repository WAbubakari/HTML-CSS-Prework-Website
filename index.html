A Little About Me

2002 I wanted a website for my breeding business so I researched HTML and built myself a website. Over
time I had other breeders ask me about my website so I ended up making webistes for several other breeders
as well. Also, aalong the way a candle maker and a baseball coach. I saw a silicone doll about a year ago
and really liked them so I researched how to make them and started making them myself since I had retired
from breeding and had some time. Now that I am retired and my youngest is school age I am ready to expand
my knowledge and make coding a career! This is the first website in my portfolio for my Computer
Engineering course at Coding Temple! Keep checking back for updates to my expantion of knowledge.
